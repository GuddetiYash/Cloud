Overview

Prompts play a crucial role in interacting with PLEXOS Intelligence, providing seamless guidance through Solutions and addressing general PLEXOS queries. Using the right prompts enables you to maximize the potential of the platform by retrieving actionable insights, simplifying workflows, and gaining a deeper understanding of PLEXOS.

 

This article includes a list of skills and example prompts designed to showcase how PLEXOS Intelligence supports various scenarios. These examples highlight some of the tasks you can perform using the PLEXOS Intelligence, offering a starting point to make the most of its capabilities.

 

The examples provided are not exhaustive. Try the prompts listed in this article or experiment with your own to discover more and save time using this innovative chat assistant.

 

 

NOTE: The PLEXOS Intelligence feature is available for both Simulate & Analytics tier customers.

Refer to the following example prompts and scenarios where you can use PLEXOS Intelligence to streamline tasks and enhance your workflow:

Get Insights on the Study Version Changes

 

[To quickly understand version-specific updates]

 Can you please specify the changes made in the last version?

PLEXOS Intelligence responds with a structured summary based on available data, including:

 

  • Summary of Changes: A high-level overview of modifications between versions.

  • Top 3 Most Frequently Changed Attributes: Highlights recurring changes across the dataset.

  • Top Modified Classes: Identifies object classes that were most impacted.

  • Interpretation and Implications: Explains what the changes might mean for your modeling or results.

  • Conclusion/ Final Notes: Wraps the insight to support informed decision-making.
What changes were made in version X?

Reduce Congestion

PLEXOS Intelligence provides actionable recommendations to reduce congestion by adjusting generator injections on congested lines. After querying initial congestion data (e.g., identifying where congestion is present), use the following prompts for targeted solutions / use the provided prompts by PLEXOS Intelligence:

 

Give me the recommendations to reduce congestion on XX line on 12 February at 10 PM.
 

 PLEXOS Intelligence introduces a Recommended Injection column, where you can view recommendations in a Grid format. These recommendations suggest scenarios where reducing generator injections can help alleviate congestion effectively.
Suggest ways to alleviate congestion on XX line on 15th Jan 2024 date at XX time. PLEXOS Intelligence introduces a Recommended Injection column, a requested Line for a requested timeline, where you can view recommendations in a Grid format. These recommendations suggest scenarios where reducing generator injections can help alleviate congestion effectively.
Provide recommendations to reduce congestion on Line XX at 2 PM on April 1st. PLEXOS Intelligence introduces a Recommended Injection column for requested Line for requested timeline, where you can view recommendations in a Grid format. These recommendations suggest scenarios where reducing generator injections can help alleviate congestion effectively.

If you are unsure about the lines, you can ask PLEXOS Intelligence to reduce congestion:

Can you try to reduce the congestion?

 PLEXOS Intelligence provides a dialogue box where you can select a congested line along with its interval (time and date). After selecting the line and interval, PLEXOS Intelligence introduces a Recommended Injection column in a grid format. This column offers actionable recommendations, suggesting scenarios where reducing generator injections can effectively alleviate congestion.
