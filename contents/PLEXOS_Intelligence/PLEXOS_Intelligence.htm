Overview

PLEXOS Intelligence is an innovative AI-driven chat assistant designed to simplify and enhance your interaction with Solutions and PLEXOS-related queries. This tool provides data-driven insights in text, grid, or chart formats and includes direct links to the Output Grid tab for deeper analysis. 

 

With PLEXOS Intelligence, you can ask questions related to the Solution data or general PLEXOS queries such as: 

 

  • What is the offtake for fuel?  
  • What is the production for emissions?  
  • What’s the difference between the MT and ST phases?  
  • What is the Parquet File Format? 

 

This feature significantly reduces the time spent manually sifting through the Data Grid tab. It allows you to ask questions and identify areas where you want to dig further into your Solution. After asking a question, you can jump to the Data Grid tab to investigate further, streamlining your workflow and enhancing decision-making. 

 

The PLEXOS Intelligence Widget is accessible from all pages of the PLEXOS Cloud, allowing you to access the information wherever you need it. You can select from predefined questions or enter custom queries to receive tailored responses that streamline your workflow and decision-making. 

 

NOTE: We do not use any customer data to train PLEXOS Intelligence without your consent. Additionally, we do not use or monitor your interactions with PLEXOS Intelligence. For any questions or further assistance, please contact your Customer Success Manager.

[6] Get Insights on the Study Version Changes

This skill allows you to ask questions about changes made in a specific Study version. When prompted, it analyzes the Input Data tab on the Data page and provides a structured summary of what changed in that version.


This capability helps you quickly understand version-specific updates, identify frequently changed elements, and interpret potential modeling impacts, making version reviews faster and more insightful.

 

When a study includes version history, you can now ask questions like:

 

  • Can you please specify the changes made in the last version?
  • What changes were made in version X?

 

PLEXOS Intelligence responds with a structured summary based on available data, including:

 

  • Summary of Changes: A high-level overview of modifications between versions.

  • Top 3 Most Frequently Changed Attributes: Highlights recurring changes across the dataset.

  • Top Modified Classes: Identifies object classes that were most impacted.

  • Interpretation and Implications: Explains what the changes might mean for your modeling or results.

  • Conclusion/ Final Notes: Wraps the insight to support informed decision-making.

[8] Get Suggestions to Resolve Simulation Infeasibility

PLEXOS Intelligence provides recommendations to help resolve infeasibility issues in your simulation runs.

➤ What Are Infeasibility Issues?

Infeasibility in simulations occurs when there are conflicting constraints or data inconsistencies that make it mathematically impossible for the solver to find a valid solution. These issues often arise due to modeling errors or conflicting operational limits, making it essential to identify and resolve them efficiently.


PLEXOS Intelligence analyzes your simulation results, including diagnostic IIS (Irreducibly Inconsistent Set) files, and offers targeted suggestions to help you resolve the infeasibility issues.


Ask PLEXOS Intelligence directly using prompts like:

 

  • Give me suggestions to fix the infeasibility for the simulation with simulation ID 12345.
  • Give me suggestions to fix the infeasibility for the last failed simulation.


Refer to the following GIF for an example:


 

 NOTE: This functionality is only available for Simulations Run using the Gurobi Solver.

 ➤ Prerequisite: Enable IIS Files

To receive meaningful suggestions, your model must generate IIS (Irreducibly Inconsistent Set) files. You can enable this option in PLEXOS Desktop:

 

  1. Open your model in PLEXOS Desktop.
  2. Go to Simulation > Diagnostics.
  3. In the Formulation section, check the box for Irreducibly Inconsistent Set (IIS).

 

Once enabled, PLEXOS Intelligence can use the IIS data to guide you through resolving infeasibility issues more effectively.

PLEXOS Intelligence in PLEXOS Desktop

PLEXOS Intelligence is also integrated into the PLEXOS Desktop version 11R03 or above. This enables you to interact with PLEXOS Intelligence directly within the PLEXOS Desktop environment, allowing for streamlined access to support, model insights, and relevant documentation, according to your subscription tier.

 

You can ask questions, receive structured responses in text, grid, or chart formats, and use direct navigation to model outputs and learning resources, all from within the desktop interface.

 

[A] Prerequisites for PLEXOS Intelligence on Desktop:

  1. Must be onboarded onto the Central License Manager (CLM).
  2. PLEXOS Desktop version 11R03 or above.
  3. Installation of the PLEXOS Cloud Toolkit from the Marketplace.
