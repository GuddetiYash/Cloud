Overview

Datahub is a collaborative storage system that helps you manage your PLEXOS and other relevant files with version control, efficient access, and simplified file management for all users within your organization. The data is managed per tenant to ensure full security and privacy, allowing teams to collaborate confidently within their own isolated data environment.Â 

Â 

You can also bring and manage your own external data files within Datahub, making it a centralized and flexible solution for simulation and modeling workflows.

ðŸ”‘ Key Features of Datahub:

  • Versioned File Support: Store and maintain multiple versions of the same file, allowing better tracking and rollback when needed.

  • Optimized Storage Utilization: Due to built-in version control, Datahub reduces redundant uploads and saves storage space, improving allocation.

  • Faster Access and Improved Productivity: Centralized access reduces retrieval time, simplifies referencing in scripts, and accelerates simulation workflows.

  • Tenant-Level Accessibility: Datahub is shared across the tenant, giving all users permissioned access to a common storage pool. By default, permissions are permissive but can be governed by tenant administrators.
