Overview

The Map Settings feature allows you to customize the map interface to suit your preferences. It provides multiple viewing modes, such as Streets, Light, Dark, Canvas, and Satellite, offering flexibility in how you interpret spatial data. This customization helps you easily analyze and visualize data within a map-based interface.

 

You can access the Map Settings from the following locations:

 

  • Data > Setup Tab


  • Solutions > Maps Tab

Map Type

The Map Type section lists different styles of Maps that you can apply in real-time. Click on a map type to see the changes reflected immediately on the Map interface. 

 

Available options include: 

 

  • Streets Mode: Provides a detailed view of roads, buildings, and points of interest, ideal for urban or regional analysis.

  • Light Mode: Displays a minimalist light-colored map, reducing visual clutter for better focus on data points.

  • Dark Mode: Offers a dark-themed map, suitable for presentations or night-time analysis to reduce glare.

  • Canvas Mode: Shows a simplified map with minimal details, useful for highlighting specific data layers without distractions.

  • Satellite Mode: Presents a satellite imagery view, ideal for analyzing geographical features and land use.

Map Details

The Map Details section offers additional toggles to enhance your map view. These options allow you to overlay specific elements or features on the map for a richer visualization experience:

 

  • Buildings: Adds 3D building shapes to the map, providing a realistic representation of urban areas.

  • Land, Water & Sky: Highlights the natural geography of the map, emphasizing landforms, water bodies, and atmospheric details.

  • Place Labels: Displays names of cities, towns, and other notable locations for better orientation.

  • Natural Features: Adds markers for geographical elements like mountains, forests, and rivers to give a comprehensive view of the environment.

  • Road Network: Shows a detailed layout of highways, streets, and paths, essential for transportation and logistics analysis.

  • Wind Arrows: Displays directional wind patterns using arrows, helpful for understanding wind flow in a given area.

  • Wind Particles: Adds animated particles to visualize wind speed and movement dynamically.

  • Wind Contour: Displays wind pressure levels on the map, ideal for studying atmospheric conditions.

 

By toggling these features on or off, you can create a customized map view tailored to your specific analysis needs.
