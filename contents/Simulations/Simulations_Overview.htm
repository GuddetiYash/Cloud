Simulations Overview

The Simulations is the execution of a model that solves complex optimization problems based on input datasets, constraints, and algorithmic configurations to produce deterministic or stochastic outcomes for energy system planning and analysis. This page helps manage run settings, monitor execution, and handle credit usage and refunds for failed runs.

Simulations in PLEXOS Cloud refer to running a model over time to evaluate how it performs under specific scenarios, such as changes in weather, demand growth, or fuel costs etc.
