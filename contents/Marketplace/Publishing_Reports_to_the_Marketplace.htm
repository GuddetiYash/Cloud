Overview

Reports in the Marketplace provide structured, data-driven insights that help users analyze and interpret complex datasets. The Marketplace supports two types of reports:

 

  1. Solution Reports: The Solution Reports are built to address common analysis scenarios and are useful as is or as a starting point to build your own Reports. These reports provide structured analysis with custom columns, totals, formulas, and calculations tailored to specific Modeling scenarios.

  2. BI Reports: Business Intelligence (BI) Reports are data-driven visualizations and analytical Reports that transform complex datasets into actionable insights. These reports help you interpret trends, monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), and make informed decisions based on structured and interactive data representations. 

    BI Reports in PLEXOS Cloud provide customizable dashboards, charts, and tables that enable you to explore Simulation results, compare scenarios, and analyze system performance efficiently.

 

You can publish Reports in the Marketplace using the following options:

 

  1. Create New Submission: Allows you to create and submit a new Report from scratch to meet your analysis needs.

  2. Add to Existing Submission: Allows you to create and publish new versions of existing Reports. This functionality is particularly useful when updates or modifications are required to existing Reports. For instance, you might need to enhance a Report by adding new content, revising data, or refining existing information. Using this feature, you can efficiently manage multiple versions of Reports while maintaining a consistent structure and history.

Publishing Reports to the Marketplace

Refer to the following step-by-step instructions on creating and publishing Reports to the Marketplace: 

★ [Step 1]: Create a New Submission

  1. Sign In to PLEXOS Cloud using your credentials.

  2. Navigate to the Solutions > Reports.

 

★ [Step 2]: Choose the Report

  1. Select the Reports you wish to add to the Marketplace from the Shared Reports or My Reports section.

  2. Click the Kebab Menu (three-dot-menu) available on each Report and select Publish To Marketplace.

  3. Click + Create New Submission.

★ [Step 3]: Submit / Publish the Playbook

If your Role is User (without any publishing permissions):

 

  1. On the Submitter panel, provide the following information:

    • Select the Content Visibility that determines who can view your submission (e.g., Private, or Tenant).

    • Enter the Report Title, Description, and Publisher Name.

    • Enter Version details and Category type (Gas, Electric, Water, Universal).

    • Upload images to support your Playbook (if any).

    • Upload Release Notes in .pdf format to state the updates.

    • Upload Miscellaneous Files (if any) in .xlsx, .xls, or .csv format.

 

  1. Click SUBMIT.

  2. The Report will be submitted to the Content Moderator for review. The Moderator evaluates the content for accuracy, completeness, and compliance. If necessary, the Moderator may edit details like playbook names, descriptions, and SKUs. Once the review is complete, the Moderator approves and publishes the content to the Marketplace.

 

If Your Role is Content Publisher: 

 

  1. On the Publisher panel, provide the following information:

    • Select the Content Visibility that determines who can view your submission (e.g., Private, My Tenant).

    • Enter Report Description and Publisher Name.

    • Upload Report-related Images to support your Report (If any).

    • Upload Release Notes in .pdf format to state the updates.

    • Upload Miscellaneous Files (if any) in .xlsx, .xls, or .csv format.
       

  2. Click PUBLISH.  

  3. The Report will be published on the Marketplace.

  4. Go to the Marketplace to preview the published content. The content published at the Tenant level is exclusively visible to users within that Tenant on the Marketplace.

    To quickly filter and view content based on its accessibility level, use the Visibility Status dropdown in the Marketplace. 

Publishing Report Versions to the Marketplace

Refer to the following step-by-step instructions on creating and publishing Playbooks to the Marketplace: 

★ [Step 1]: Add To Existing Submission

  1. Sign In to PLEXOS Cloud using your credentials.

  2. Navigate to the Solutions > Reports.

 

★ [Step 2]: Choose the Report

  1. Select the Reports you wish to add to your Playbook from the Shared Reports or My Reports section.

  2. Click the Kebab Menu (three-dot-menu) available on each Report and select Publish To Marketplace.

  3. Click + Add To Existing Submission.

★ [Step 3]: Add Version Details

  1. Select the Report to which you wish to link the new submission.


  2. Enter the Version Number in the following format:

    •    X.X or X.X.X (For example: 1.0 or 1.0.1)

★ [Step 4]: Submit / Publish the Playbook

If your Role is User (without any publishing permissions):

 

  1. On the Submitter panel, provide the following information:

    • Select the Content Visibility that determines who can view your submission (e.g., Private, or Tenant).

    • Enter the Report  Description and Publisher Name.

    • Upload images to support your Report (if any).

    • Upload Release Notes in .pdf format to state the updates.

    • Upload Miscellaneous Files (if any) in .xlsx, .xls, or .csv format.
       

 

  1. Click SUBMIT.

  2. The Report will be submitted to the Content Moderator for review.   The Moderator evaluates the content for accuracy, completeness, and compliance. If necessary, the Moderator may edit details like playbook names, descriptions, and SKUs. Once the review is complete, the Moderator approves and publishes the content to the Marketplace.

 

If Your Role is Content Publisher: 

 

  1. On the Publisher panel, provide the following information:

    • Select the Content Visibility that determines who can view your submission (e.g., Private, Tenant, or Public).

    • Enter Playbook Description and Publisher Name.

    • Upload Images to support your Report (If any).

    • Upload Release Notes in .pdf format to state the updates.

    • Upload Miscellaneous Files (if any) in .xlsx, .xls, or .csv format.


  2. Click PUBLISH.  

  3. The Report will be published on the Marketplace.

  4. Go to the Marketplace to preview the published content. The content published at the Tenant level is exclusively visible to users within that Tenant on the Marketplace.

    To quickly filter and view content based on its accessibility level, use the Visibility Status dropdown in the Marketplace.
