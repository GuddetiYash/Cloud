Frequently Asked Questions

Q 1. What is the Energy Exemplar Marketplace, and how does it help?

Answer: The Energy Exemplar Marketplace is a centralized platform for hosting and managing products such as Playbooks, Reports, Excel Templates, and Tools. It simplifies the content lifecycle by enabling seamless creation, publishing, version control, and collaboration. This ensures high-quality outputs and efficient workflows across various teams and regions.

Q 2. What types of content are available on the Marketplace?

Answer: The Marketplace hosts diverse content, including:

  1. Playbooks: For Modeling and analysis tasks.
  2. Reports: BI Reports and Solution Reports for insights and evaluations.
  3. Templates: Excel Add-in templates for consistent data handling.
  4. Tools: PLEXOS Desktop Installer, Cloud Toolkits, and Python SDKs.

Q 3. How can I copy content to my Cloud enviornment from the Marketplace?

Answer: To copy content to your tenant:

  1. Locate the desired content on the Marketplace.
  2. Click ADD TO CLOUD on the content card.
  3. Select a desired Version and enter the Study Name.
  4. Click CONFIRM.
  5. You’ll receive an email notification once the content is successfully copied.

Q 4. What are the key user roles in the Marketplace, and what are their responsibilities?

Answer: The key user roles include:

  1. Content Publisher: Creates and publishes content to the Tenant level.
  2. Content Moderator: Reviews and approves submitted content from other users at the Tenant level and publishes to the Marketplace.

Q 5. How do I publish Playbooks to the Marketplace?

Answer: To publish Playbooks:

  1. Navigate to the Marketplace and click CREATE PLAYBOOK.
  2. Select an appropriate Study Version and add content such as Executive Views or BI Reports.
  3. Submit for moderation (if your role is User) or publish directly (if you’re a Content Publisher).

Q 6. Can I publish a new version of an existing Playbook? How?

Answer: Yes, to publish a new version:

 

  1. Click CREATE PLAYBOOK > Add To Existing Submission.
  2. Select the existing Playbook and specify the new Version Number.
  3. Add updated content and submit for review or publish directly, based on your role.

Q 7. How can I manage or edit my published submissions?

Answer: To edit published content:

  1. Go to My Content under your Profile.
  2. Find the content and click the Kebab Menu (three-dot menu).
  3. Edit details like Title, Description, or Publisher Name, adjust visibility, or add licenses.
  4. Click SAVE to apply changes.

Q 8. How do I publish Excel Templates and Tools to the Marketplace?

Answer: To publish:

  1. Go to the Marketplace and click Add Content.
  2. For Excel Templates, set the Content-Type to Template and Category to Excel Templates.
  3. For Tools, set the Content-Type to Tools and Category to Others.
  4. Provide details, upload files (e.g., Excel Add-in or ZIP files), and submit for review.

Q 9. How can I search and filter content in the Marketplace?

Answer: The Marketplace provides robust search and filter options:

  1. Use filters like Date Range, Visibility Status, or Sorting Options (e.g., Newest, Most Downloaded).
  2. Apply these filters to quickly locate specific content across Playbooks, Reports, Templates, and Tools.
