Overview

The Storage Management page under Admin focuses on managing storage across all Studies and Solutions to assist admins in managing storage in their tenants. This page is configured to display a list of all Studies and Solutions, including Archived and Unarchived. It allows you to Archive, Restore, and Delete single or multiple Studies and Solutions (up to 10 at a time).

 

Storage Details panel

The Storage Details panel, which is available on the right side of the Storage Management page, replaces the previous storage metrics shown at the top of the page. This panel provides a comprehensive overview of your tenant’s storage usage, featuring a Summary bar and detailed information on Active, Archived, and Available Storage. These details assist tenant Admins in understanding their storage usage, getting tips to manage storage effectively, and planning for future needs.

 

Below is the summary of the Storage Details panel:

  • Storage Summary Bar - The summary bar visually represents the tenant's total storage usage, displaying the proportions of Active, Archived, and Available storage. This allows you to understand your tenant’s storage at a glance and save time by accessing the most relevant information. 

     

  • Active Storage - Displays the amount of storage currently in use for your tenant’s active data in terabytes (TB) and as a percentage (%).

  • Archived Storage - Displays the Storage consumed by Studies and Solutions once they are Archived. Studies and Solutions archived do not appear in PLEXOS Cloud but will still consume Storage unless deleted.

  • Available Storage - Displays the remaining storage capacity in terabytes (TB) and as a percentage (%) after accounting for active and archived data consumption.

An Alert Message will appear in the Storage Details panel when your tenant’s storage capacity reaches or exceeds 95%.  This Alert Message encourages you to take appropriate actions to manage your tenant storage effectively.

Tips to Reduce Storage

This section provides a few practical tips to tenant Admins to help avoid overconsuming storage. These tips include deleting unnecessary Studies and Solutions, setting a scheduler for automatic Archival and Deletion of Studies and Solutions after a set number of days, cleaning Archived Storage data, etc. It also includes a link to the STORAGE tab under the Admin > Tenant Preferences page, where you can manage your tenant storage.

Studies Tab

See the list of columns below to learn more about the actions you can perform within this tab:

 NOTE: Only the Admin user has access to the Storage Management tab and can update the Study details for their organization.

 

1. Study Name: Lists the name of the Study.

2. Size (GB): Shows the actual size of the Study in gigabytes (GB).

3. Created By: Shows the name of the user who created the Study.

4. Owner: By default, a user who created the Study will be the owner of that Study. An admin can change the owner using the Change Owner button in the upper-right corner of the screen.

5. Created On: Shows the date and time the Study was created.

6. Last Updated Date: Shows the date and time the last update was made to the Study.

7. Members: Shows the number of users who have access to the Study. Public Studies will show the number of total licensed users in the system.

8. Action: Allows you to Restore or Delete single or multiple Studies at once.


There are three bulk action buttons above the grid on the top right. They allow the administrator to Change the owner, Archive or Delete one or multiple Studies (up to 10 at a time).

 

How to Delete a Study from PLEXOS Cloud?

To delete a Study from PLEXOS Cloud you must first Archive the Study using the steps provided below:
 

Archive a Study

Archiving a Study removes it from the Study dropdown at the top of the screen. This reduces clutter, follows company auditing practices, and prevents users from accessing Studies that are no longer applicable. Once archived the Study can be Restored from within Administration or deleted.

NOTE: The owner of a Study can archive that Study, but only administrators can delete Studies.

 

1.  From the PLEXOS Cloud homepage, click on the Study dropdown and navigate to a Study you want to delete.

2.  Click on the kebab icon (three vertical dots) and select Archive. An Archived Study is removed from the main interface and navigation.

 


3.  A confirmation pop-up displays, click YES to continue. This will Archive the Study and removes it from the Study dropdown list.

 NOTE: Select Delete all Solutions in this Study checkbox to delete all the solutions permanently. This action cannot be undone so be sure to download all required Artifacts before selecting this option.

 


  -OR-

Go to ADMIN > Storage Management tab, select one or more Unarchived Studies  (Use Action column filter to view Unarchived Studies), and click on the Archive Study icon in the upper-right corner of the window.
 

 

Delete a Study

Deleting a Study removes it from PLEXOS Cloud freeing up storage space in your tenant. Once deleted there is no ability to recover the study.

 NOTE: Deleting Studies can only be done by users who have Administration access.

 

1.  Click on the ADMIN page.


2.  Select the Storage Management tab to view the list of all Studies including Archived and Unarchived Studies.
 

3.  To find an Archived Study, hover over the Actions column header name and click on the hamburger menu  (three horizontal lines) icon to pop-up the filter options.
 

4.  By default, Unarchived and Archived checkboxes are selected, deselect the Unarchived checkbox to view a list of only Archived Studies.

5.  Select the desired Study from the list.

NOTE: You can select a maximum of 10 Studies for deletion at once.

6.  Click on the Delete icon in the Actions column.
 

7.  A confirmation dialog displays, click YES to delete the Study.
 


NOTE: If you have bulk studies selected, click the Delete icon in the upper-right corner of the screen to delete all the selected Studies.

 

 
NOTE: If you have selected Archived and Unarchived Studies together, a confirmation pop-up displays stating, “Please note that some of the studies are not archived and will not be deleted. Please confirm you want to proceed with deletion of the selected Archived studies?”

 

 
 NOTE: If you have selected Unarchived Study for deletion, you will see an error, “None of the selected Studies are archived. Hence cannot be deleted.”

 

Unarchive a Study

Unarchiving a Study moves the Study back into the main navigation. The Study can be viewed by all users that have access to it.

1.  To Unarchive an Archived Study, navigate or select the desired Study from the list.

2.  Click on the Unarchive Study  icon.
 

3.  A confirmation pop-up displays, click Yes to restore the Archived Study. Once unarchived the Study will show up again in the Study dropdown on the main page and be accessible by all users who previously had access to that Study.
 

 
NOTE: You can only Unarchive one study at once.


Change a Study Owner

The Change Owner option enables the admin user to change and assign the owner rights of a single or multiple Studies to any user in their organization. The Study Owner controls who has access to the Study. Changing the owner is useful if someone is on leave or has left the team and a different user should not control adding/removing members for the Study.

NOTE: As an admin, you cannot change & assign yourself as an Owner of a Study. Another Admin can assign you as the Owner. This is a security limitation to prevent admins from being able to access Studies that have not been granted access by the Owner.

To change the Owner of a Study,

 

1.  Select the Study or Studies that you want to adjust by clicking the checkbox at the far left of the grid.


2.  Click on the CHANGE OWNER option located at upper-right corner of the window.
 

3.  In the Change Owner search box, type the name of user or click the dropdown to view the list of users.
 

4.  After selecting the desired user, click on the CHANGE button.
 

5.  Next, a pop-up displays with a confirmation message, click the CONFIRM button and wait for the changes to execute.

Solutions Tab

The Solutions tab is configured to display the list of all Solutions including archived and unarchived with its associated information such as Triggered By, Triggered At, Storage, and Execution Id. It allows you to Archive, Unarchive, and Delete single or multiple Solutions (up to 5 at a time).

See the list of columns below to learn more about the actions you can perform within this tab:

 

1. Study Name: Lists the name of the Study.

2. Models: Lists the name of Models associated with Solutions. If there are multiple Models for one Solution, it will be shown in a comma separated format.
 

3. Triggered At: Shows the date and time the Solution was triggered.

4. Triggered By: Shows the name of the person who triggered the Solution.

5. Storage: Shows the actual size of the Solution in gigabytes (GB). By default, the Storage column displays the Solutions size in descending order. You can hover over the column name and use the arrow   icon to switch between ascending and descending order.

6. Execution Id: Shows a unique execution Id for different Solutions.

7. Action: Allows you to Archive single or multiple Solutions at once. Hover over column name and click three horizontal lines to pop up the filter. Using Filter, you can configure (add or remove) the columns to the table.

There are three bulk action buttons above the grid on the top right. They allow the administrator to Unarchive, Archive or Delete one or multiple Solutions (up to 5 at a time).

 

How to Delete a Solution(s) in PLEXOS Cloud?

To delete a Solution(s) from PLEXOS Cloud you must first Archive the Solution using the steps provided.
 

Archive a Solution(s)

NOTE: The Owner of a Study can Archive the Study, but only Administrators can delete Solution(s).

 

1.  Navigate to ADMIN > Storage Management tab > Solutions.
 


2.  To find an Archived Solution from the list, hover over the Actions Column header name and click on the three horizontal lines   icon to pop-up the filter options.
 


3.  By default, Unarchived and Archived checkboxes are selected, deselect the Unarchived checkbox to view a list of only Archived Solutions.


 NOTE: You can select a maximum of 10 Solutions for deletion at once.

4.  Select the desired active Solution (not archived) from the list and click on the Archive   icon in the Action column.

 
-OR-

5.  If you are selecting multiple active Solutions, click on the Archive  icon in the upper right corner of the window to Archive all in one click.
 
6.  A confirmation pop-up displays, click YES to continue.
 

Delete a Solution(s)

Deleting a Solution(s) removes it from PLEXOS Cloud freeing up storage space in your tenant. Once deleted there is no ability to recover the Solution.

NOTE

: Deleting Solution(s) can only be done by users who have Administration access.

 

1.  In the Solutions tab, find and select the Archived Solution(s) from the list. Use the Actions column filter to sort Archived Solutions.


NOTE: You can select a maximum of 10 Solutions for deletion at once.
 


2.  Click on the Delete icon in the Actions column.



3.  A confirmation dialog displays, click YES to delete the Solution.



NOTE: If you have bulk Solutions selected, click the Delete icon in the upper-right corner of the screen to delete all the selected Solutions.

 


NOTE: If you have selected Archived and Unarchived Solutions together, a confirmation pop-up displays stating, “Please confirm you want to proceed with deletion of the selected Solutions?"

 

Manually Archive/Delete Solutions from the Solutions Card

Users can now manually Delete and Archive Solutions from Solutions card. This will help users manage storage and eliminate unnecessary Solutions so they can focus on the Solutions that matter.

NOTE: The manual Archive and Delete options are only available for users with a Modeler License. This is not available for Read Only users.
 
  1. Navigate to Solutions tab.
     
  2. Find and open any required Solution to Archive or Delete.
     
  3. Hover in the Solutions card and click on the kebab menu (three vertical dots).
     
 
  1. Select Archive or Delete option for your Solution.

NOTE: If you click on Archive, it will Archive the Solution without showing any confirmation dialog. Once archived, Administrators can view and unarchive these Solutions under ADMIN > Storage Management tab.

-OR-

If you click on Delete, you will see a confirmation dialog before Deleting a Solution. The action is permanent, and the deleted solution cannot be retrieved again.

 
 

Manually Archive a Study from the Study Dropdown

An Admin user or the Study Owner can use the Study dropdown to manually Archive a Study and delete its associated Solutions. This option is designed to make it faster for users to free up storage space.



Follow the steps below to manually Archive a Study from Study dropdown:
 
  1. Click on the Study dropdown and scroll to find the desired Study.
     
  2. Click on the kebab menu (three vertical dots) available next to the Study name.
 

 
  1. Select Archive option.
 
  1. A confirmation dialog displays as shown below:
     
 
  1. Selecting the highlighted checkbox above will delete all Solutions associated with this Study. Once deleted, the Solutions cannot be retrieved again.
     
  2. Click Yes to continue.
     
  3. After a Study is successfully Archived, you will see a notification at the top.
     
  1. The Study will remain in an archived state until an Administrator deletes it manually or the automated storage tools delete it.
     
Admin users can perform the similar action for any Study in their tenant under Admin > Storage Management tab. 




 

Restore or Unarchive a Solution(s)

1.  If you need to use a Solution in the interface that you previously archived, you can restore it. To Restore an Archived Solution, navigate and select the desired Solutions(s) from the list. Use the Actions column filter to sort Archived solutions.
 

2.  Click on the Unarchive  icon.

3.  A confirmation pop-up displays, click YES to restore the Archived Solution. Once restored the Solution will be accessible by all users who previously had access to that Solution.


4.  For bulk deletion of Solutions, select the required Archived solutions and click on the Unarchive icon available in the right-hand side of the screen.

NOTE: You can Unarchive only 10 Solutions at a time.
 
 

Export all your Studies and Solutions

An Export icon is available for Admin users in the Admin > Storage Management tab (above the Action column). It allows Admins to export the data from the grid in either .csv or .xlsx format. It is helpful to get this data to sort filters, groups, etc. Also, you can use these data as a backup for future reference or can share it within your internal teams for analytics purposes.

 

NOTE: You must switch between the Studies and Solutions tab to download individual data.
