Overview

The Tenant Preferences page allows administrators to change settings/preferences that impact, what users see in the Tenant. An Admin can view all Engines available for the Tenant and decide which to show or hide for all users. Additionally, the Admin can replace the PLEXOS Cloud Logo at the top left of the interface with a company Logo in a variety of supported image formats, Manage Storage with auto Archival and Deletion of Solutions over a specific time period, Set Maximum Cores and Credits for Tenant, and prevent triggering new Simulation with zero Credits.
 


To navigate to the Tenant Preferences page:


1. Click on the Admin button at the bottom of the Primary Navigation bar.
 

2. Click on the Tenant Preference page to view the page on the right-hand section of the screen.
 
 

3. The Tenant Preference page has four tabs within it.. Please find the description for each tab in detail as mentione below:

 

GENERAL Tab

This section allows Admins to restrict users from triggering new Simulations with zero Credits and manage the maximum number of Cores and Credits for users in their Tenant.
 

Prevent Users from triggering Simulations with zero Credits

NOTE: This feature applies to customers with a "Usage-based" pricing model.

Admins can restrict users from triggering new Simulations when the Available Compute Credits are less than or equal to zero. This helps the Administrator to control the organization's budget by preventing overruns. By default, the toggle button is enabled, allowing users to trigger new Simulations even after the Available Compute Credits are less than or equal to zero. 

To prevent all tenant users from triggering new Simulations when the Available Compute Credits are 0 or less, the Tenant Admin must disable the setting and click on the SAVE button to apply the changes.


For Production customers, the checkbox will be enabled, allowing them to Run a Simulation even if their Credits are zero or below. In this case, any overages are required to be paid based on the terms of the contract.

For trial Tenant customers, the checkbox will be disabled by default, and they cannot enable it. But they can connect with the EE Support team or CSMs to purchase more Credits.

For New Production Customers, the checkbox will be disabled by default but they can enable it, if required to Run a Simulation even if the Credits are zero or below. In this case, any overages are required to be paid based on the terms of the contract.

NOTE: You must click on the SAVE button to apply the changes.
 

ENGINES Tab

Shows the engines that are available to the users in the tenant for running simulations from the PLEXOS Cloud interface, CLI, API, Excel Add-In, etc. See the columns below for more information and perform the action:

 
  • Display Name- Shows the name of an engine.
  • Version- Shows the version of an engine.  
  • Engine Type- Shows the type of engine as Performance or Standard.
  • Description- Shows when an engine was built or any other details
  • Actions- click  icon to hide an engine or click icon to show an engine. If you hide an engine, it won’t show or be available to users to select for running a model.


NOTE: Hover over the column name and click the three horizontal lines  icon to open the filter. It allows you to search, filter, and configure the table per your needs.

 

 

LOGO Tab

In this tab, you can upload the company-specific logo file that replaces the existing PLEXOS Cloud logo. Also, you can configure or remove the logo file per your requirements.


NOTE: The supported image format for uploading a Logo file includes, SVG, JPEG, JPG, and PNG.

 

                          1. Drag and drop- Drag and drop or click browse to upload your file.



2. Resize the image - Use the + and - scale to set the image size to match your requirements.

                     3. Change the position of the image - Click and drag the image (within dotted line box) to the desired position.

 


 

After making the necessary changes to your file, click Upload to save the changes. You can view the updated logo in the upper left corner of the screen. This will be available to all users on all screens within the PLEXOS Cloud interface.


 

STORAGE Tab

The Storage tab allows you to manage auto Archival and Deletion of Solutions over a specific time period. This helps to manage storage by tracking the unused Solutions created in the past years and configuring them for Archival and Deletion per your requirement. The Storage tab is divided into three sections as shown below:

 

 

  • Solutions: Allows you to select the number of latest Solutions per Model. So, when a new Solution is created, the oldest Solution will be automatically Archived.

    NOTE: Only the Successful Solution will be considered under this section. The value in the Solutions dropdown ranges from 10 to 50. There is a cool off period of 24 hours meaning, any new Solution which is recently created will not be considered for Auto-Archival for the next 24 hours even if it falls under the criteria of Auto-Archival. 

    For example, if the Auto-Archival process is scheduled to begin at 1 AM today, it will consider the Solutions available for Archival before 1 AM yesterday (following a 24-hour cool-off period).  
     
  •  Failed Solutions: This section allows you to automatically delete Solutions derived from Failed, Cancelled, and Completed with Error Simulations after a specific period selected in the Days dropdown.

    NOTE: The values in the Days dropdown are 7, 14, and 30 days.
  • Archived Studies & Solutions: This section allows you to delete the Archived Studies and Solutions after a specific period selected in the Days dropdown.

  • Reset: Click the RESET button to restore the configuration to the last saved changes.

  • Save: Click the SAVE button to apply the configuration changes. Each section has a default value; if you wish to have the same value, simply click SAVE directly.
