Update Your Password

How to update a forgotten password or change your password in PLEXOS Cloud account settings.

Reset a Forgotten Password

  1. Go to the PLEXOS Cloud log in screen.
  2. Enter your email address and click Continue.
  3. Click Forgot Password?.
  4. Enter your registered email address and click Submit. You will receive an email with a link to reset your password.

 

 NOTE: The above process is only applicable for Non-SSO (Single Sign-On) login users.

Update your password in PLEXOS Cloud

  1. Log in to PLEXOS Cloud.
  2. Click your username in the top-right corner of the screen and select Update Password.
  3. Enter a new password, confirm the new password, and click Submit.
