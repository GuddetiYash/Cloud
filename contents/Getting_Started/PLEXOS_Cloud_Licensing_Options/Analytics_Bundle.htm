Analytics Bundle

The Analytics Bundle is designed to provide users with enhanced features for a deeper understanding of their output data. With the Analytics Bundle, along with the ability to run the Simulations users also gain access to analyze the output on deeper level with Map Visualizations, ad hoc queries (Charts, Grid, Reports), the Excel-AddIn (for editing input data, and analyzing output data), powerful BI Analytics capabilities, Event Detection for advanced Reporting and dashboarding in PLEXOS Cloud.
Analytics Bundle unlocks the full potential of the BI Analytics reporting feature.  BI Analytics is exclusively available with the PLEXOS Cloud platform and cannot be added to standalone PLEXOS desktop or PLEXOS Cloud Workspace opportunities. It is a unique offering to enrich your experience with comprehensive insights.

 

Licensing Options with Analytics Bundle

The Analytics Bundle is priced on a per-user basis, giving you the flexibility to select the licensing option that aligns with your requirements. We provide three user tiers, categorized by user count: for up to five users, up to ten users, and beyond ten users. This adaptable pricing framework guarantees that you only pay for the resources you require, delivering both cost-effectiveness and scalability as your team expands.


Type of User Licenses

 

Within the Analytics Bundle of PLEXOS Cloud platform, there are three distinct user types to cater to different roles in the organization:

 

  1. Analysts: Analysts are the modelers who have full access to the application. They can edit, model, execute Simulations, and create and view Reports based on the platform tier licensed. 

  2.  Reviewers: Reviewers have non-execution licenses available in blocks of five, with tailored pricing. Although Simulations are not allowed with this license, Reviewers can log in to PLEXOS Cloud and access all data, including input and output. They can leverage Solution Output Analysis tools, view Map-based visualizations, access Solution Reports, and build ad-hoc charts on the data grid.  They do not have editing capabilities or the ability to create BI Reports.

  3. Executives: Executives also have non-execution licenses, offered in blocks of five with corresponding pricing. Like Reviewers, they cannot perform Simulations but have access to view all output data and reports that are shared with them. They can explore Map-Based Visualizations, and access published Solution Reports and BI Reports. However, Executives are unable to edit input data, run Simulations, or generate reports.

  • Insights: Provides the ability to gain valuable insights and recommendations tailored to your business needs, empowering you to make informed decisions.

  • Services: Users can choose from a variety of services to support their journey, including training programs, premier support, and other specialized services.


The Analytics Bundle, along with the above listed add-ons, ensures that you have the flexibility and resources to thrive in an increasingly data-driven world.
