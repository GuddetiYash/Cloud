Overview

The Preferences page in PLEXOS Cloud allows you to configure settings such as Time and Date Preferences, Application Preferences, Email Preferences, BI Analytics Report Settings, and Display Settings. This ensures increased customizability and productivity to manage your account better.

 

Accessing the Preferences Page

Refer to the following steps to access the Preferences page:

 

  1. Sign in to PLEXOS Cloud with your credentials.


  2. Navigate to the top-right corner and click your Profile Name or Avatar.


     
  3. From the dropdown menu, select Preferences.



    The Preferences Page is now displayed and divided into sections for a better user experience.

Components of the Preferences Page

Refer to the following steps to access the Preferences page:

 

The Preferences Page in PLEXOS Cloud is organized into key sections, each addressing specific functionalities.

 

  1. User's Profile
  2. Time and Date Preferences
  3. Application Preferences
  4. Email Preferences
  5. Diagnostic
  6. BI Analytics Report Settings
  7. Display Settings

 

Following are the sections on the Preferences Page that are described in detail:

[A] User's Profile

The User's Profile section displays basic information about your account.

  • Name: Displays the name associated with your account.
  • Email Address: Displays your registered email address.

 

[B] Time and Date Preferences

The Time & Date Preferences section allows you to configure essential settings such as Time Zone, Date Format, Time Format, and Number Format to align with your regional and personal preferences. These settings ensure accurate scheduling, reporting, and numerical display across the PLEXOS Cloud platform.


Configurable Settings:

 

  • Time Zone: Select the appropriate time zone for your region from the dropdown list. This setting ensures that all timestamps align with your local time.
  • Date Format: Select a date format (e.g., 14 Jan 2025 or 2025-01-14) to match your preferred style.
  • Time Format: Choose between a 12-hour (AM/PM) or 24-hour format for better readability and consistency.
  • Number Format: Define how numbers appear in your reports, such as using commas or periods for decimal separation.

[C] Application Preferences

Favorite Studies:

This section lists your favorite studies for quick access.
If no studies are marked as favorites, it will display: “No favorite studies! Please favorite some studies to see them here.”

Adding Favorite Studies:

Marking a study as a favorite provides quick and convenient access to frequently used studies, saving time and improving productivity.

To add a study as your favorite, perform the following steps to add studies to your favorites:

  1. Log in to PLEXOS Cloud with your credentials.
  2. Navigate to the top-center and click on Study drop-down menu.
    A list of Studies appears in the dropdown
  3. From the list of available Studies, mark your preferred study as a favorite by clicking the star next to its name.

 


Studies marked as favorites appear in the Preferences section under Application Preferences.
 

[D] Email Preferences

This section allows you to configure email notifications for simulations, credits, and study-related activities.

[I] Simulations

  1. Simulation Status Notifications:
    Toggle the Simulation Status button to turn notifications for simulation activities on or off.
    Check the boxes for the status of the simulation  you want to be notified about:
    • In progress
    • Successful
    • Cancelled
    • Completed with error
    • Failed

       

  2. Long Running Simulations:
    Enable notifications for simulations exceeding 24 hours by toggling this option.

 

[II] Credits

  1. Credit Refund Alert:
    Toggle this button to receive notifications when credits are refunded due to failed simulations.

 

[III] Studies

  1. Version Rollback Notifications:
    Enable notifications for successful or failed version rollbacks by toggling this button.
    Check the boxes for the events you want to be notified about:
    • Successful 
    • Failed

       
  2. New Study From Version Notification:
    To get updates for studies created from specific versions, toggle this button.
    Check the boxes for the events you want to be notified about:
    • Successful 
    • Failed

 

[E] Diagnostic

[I] Enable Diagnostic:

Simulation Diagnostics include the Study ID, Simulation ID, Execution ID, Changeset ID, and other relevant details in the Simulation Diagnostic.

Turn diagnostics ON or OFF:

  • Yes: Allows diagnostics.
  • No: Disables diagnostics.

 

[F] BI Analytics Report Settings

[I] Auto-Save Reports:

Toggle the button to enable automatic saving of BI Analytics reports.

You can select the auto- save intervals from the following options:

  • 5 minutes
  • 10 minutes
  • 15 minutes
  • 30 minutes
  • 60 minutes

 

[G] Display Settings

[I] Solution Display

You can choose your default page from the following options to view your solution data:

  • Map
  • Grid
  • Solution Reports
  • BI Analytics

 

[II] Grid Display

You can select your preferred grid size for the Solution data in the Grid tab from the following available options:

  • Default (Large)
  • Medium
  • Compact

 

NOTE: You cannot save changes manually. They are saved automatically.
